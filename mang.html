Mangalore Culture

Mangalore ( mangaluru ) which is often described as the Gate way of Karnataka or the port city is surrounded by Arabian sea on West and Western ghats on East. Mangalore is also called as mangaluru ( in Kannada ), kudla ( in tulu ) , Kodiyal (in konkani ) , Maikala ( in beary ) , Mangalaapuram (in Malayalam), Kodeyaala (in Havyakka ), Manjarun (in Sanskrit) , Mayngalur(by our express bus conductors xD ) Mangalore is named after Mangaladevi temple . Mangalore is the only city in the world that has 9 names.
